About

About Me,

My name is Rivibibu Prabashwara, and I'm currently studying at an advanced level. I study whatever catches my interest. Sometimes, I find ideas that align with mine, and other times, they're different. I appreciate both because encountering new ideas encourages growth. I believe in the importance of change and learning something new every day. For me, becoming a better version of myself means constantly evolving and improving.

This website isn't particularly special. It's just like many others—a random person sharing thoughts and beliefs. But I think if we can share different ideas, we can become better versions of ourselves. :)

I've seen monkeys struggling for food and humans striving to be their best.
